Nasi Goreng

Description

Nasi Goreng is an Indonesian cuisine served as lunch and dinner.

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Two eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • MSG
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Put the rice in the pan
  2. Fry it, it's called fried rice, what do you expect?
  3. Fry the eggs
  4. Put some MSG on them eggs and rice
  5. Add soy sauce
  6. Voila
